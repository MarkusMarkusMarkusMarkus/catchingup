#19

Cambrian Explosion

Back in the day the Internet was the Wild Wild West. Everyone was doing whatever, making their own websites with their own aesthetics, nothing was centralized.

Then massive behemoth platforms came along and there was a massive “flattening” of online expression. Everyone got steamrolled. Playing in the internet now required — gulp — skill.

Thankfully, AI is lowering the cost of immanentizing creativity: going from idea to execution is no longer (as) skill-bound.

And the consequences are all around you.
