#10

We Are All Kids Now

Many years ago, this anime artist — Thomas Romain — went viral for turning his son's doodles into anime characters.

And I loved it so much, for two reasons:

  1. I found it so respectful and encouraging of the kid, and
  2. you can see how, via technique, the children's imagination is unbound. It beats any animation studio.

You can probably see where this is going: we are all kids now, and AI is our father.
