Becoming Unbounded

“Becoming Unbounded”

Earlier I said that I “got it”. But what was it that I got, exactly?

This is what I got:

Becoming unbounded. Made with tldraw.

Before capacity to produce was constrained by skill. You had limited time and limited skills and thus no time to bring all ideas to life.

The “skill issue” gap.

You had to make a choice: which ideas do I skill up for? "Do I learn to code or learn to design?” “Do I become a doctor or an astronaut?"

We couldn’t all be Jonny Kim. Google.

Now? No more. GenAI solves the skill issue gap: if you can dream it, you can make it.

The only constraint is now time.
