Single-player Mode

I've discussed before how companies are matryoshka dolls except instead of dolls its black boxes: the upper level gives words and money to the lower level who produce some output and so on, recursively.

So you end up with org charts that looks somewhat like this:

tldraw.

Now, company creation, too, follows a power-law: most people own zero companies. A few own one. And a tiny sliver owns many. Sure, great industrialists, Elon Musk, but I mean even in indie startup terms: just think of Pieter Levels that, some 12 years ago (https://x.com/levelsio/status/2037703173251408243?s=20), launched 12 startups in 12 months. Just him, and his laptop, all running online.

That logic can now be extended with agents: just substitute the human black box for the agent black box and feed it electricity instead of money.

A solo operator with AI can now do what previously required a small company: research, writing, customer support, data analysis, design, financial modelling, what have you.

Pieter was just early.
