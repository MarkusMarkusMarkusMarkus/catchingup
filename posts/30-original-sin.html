#29

Original Sin

There’s an old Portuguese saying — “Ladrão que rouba ladrão tem 100 anos de perdão”

i.e. thief that steals from a thief deserves 100 years of forgiveness.

Back in February Anthropic sent out three tweets accusing labs of attempting to illegally distill their models.

What was most striking was that the most liked responses to each of those tweets all sided with the attackers, following a logic from the Portuguese saying above.

I think this has never happened before: people have pushed back against companies' IP claims, but never on the grounds that the company had no moral right to what it produced from the outset, because of how it produced it.

Illegal But not immoral?
