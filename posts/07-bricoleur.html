Bricoleur

“Bricoleur”

We’ve talked a bit about my first two Midjourney projects.

Although those mostly  failed, they did end up generating something useful — almost by mistake.

As I generated sometimes I’d generate interesting images or have random thoughts. I put those to be generated as well. In doing so I ended  up with characters that didn’t fit what I was making, but that could fit something else. Maybe their own comics?

I had already played with making comics in 2024 and 2025 with ChatGPT. Character consistency was a huge problem. It still is, for Midjourney. But for a few panels it doesn’t really make a difference.

I already had the workflow, from back in 2024: just put them into Google Slides to make the panels and voilá!

In entrepreneurship “bricolage” is the idea of building from whatever is at hand. As opposed to an engineer who designs from scratch with the right tools.

I was trying to be an engineer — with my overengineered projects — when what actually worked was to shift my mindset — to become a bricoleur.
